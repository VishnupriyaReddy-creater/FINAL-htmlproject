1. MISMATCHED SEASON3


Mismatched is an Indian Hindi-language coming-of-age romantic drama television series on Netflix, based on Sandhya Menon's 2017 novel When Dimple Met Rishi. It was adapted by Gazal Dhaliwal and directed by Akarsh Khurana and Nipun Dharmadhikari. It is produced by Ronnie Screwvala's RSVP Movies.[1] Starring Prajakta Koli, Rohit Saraf, Rannvijay Singha and Vidya Malvade in prominent roles, the series is about Rishi, a die-hard romantic who believes in traditional ways of dating, who falls for Dimple, a gamer, and eventually wants to marry her.

2.ARYA 2


Arya 2 is a 2009 Indian Telugu-language psychological romantic action film co-written and directed by Sukumar. Produced by Aditya Babu and B. V. S. N. Prasad under the Aditya Arts banner, the film stars Allu Arjun in the titular role, alongside Kajal Aggarwal and Navdeep with music composed by Devi Sri Prasad. It serves as a spiritual sequel to Arya (2004).[1] The film explores the complex love-hate relationship between two childhood friends, Arya and Ajay, and their mutual love for Geetha, set against a backdrop of corporate and family conflicts.

3.MAA INTI BANGARAM


Maa Inti Bangaram is a Telugu family drama that highlights the importance of love, relationships, and values within a middle-class household. The story revolves around the emotional bond shared by family members and how mutual understanding and sacrifice keep the family united during difficult times.

4.ROWDY BOYS


Rowdy Boys is a 2022 Indian Telugu-language coming of age romantic college drama film written and directed by Sree Harsha Konuganti[1] Screen play by Chiranjiv Makwana and produced by Dil Raju and Shirish through Sri Venkateswara Creations.[2] The film features debutant Ashish Reddy, son of Dil Raju's brother, Sirish Reddy, and Anupama Parameswaran in lead roles[3] with music composed by Devi Sri Prasad.[4] The film was released theatrically on 14 January 2022.[5] The film received mixed reviews from critics.

My Favourite Tourist Spots

BEACHES

Beaches are another favourite tourist spot as they provide a refreshing and joyful experience. The sound of waves, soft sand, and cool sea breeze create a relaxing mood for visitors. Beaches are perfect for spending time with friends and family, enjoying sunsets, walking along the shore, and participating in activities like swimming and boating. The beauty of the ocean and the open sky gives a sense of freedom and happiness, making beach destinations enjoyable for people of all ages.


HILL STATIONS

Hill stations are one of my favourite tourist spots because they offer a peaceful environment, cool climate, and beautiful natural scenery. Surrounded by green mountains, misty clouds, and fresh air, hill stations are perfect for relaxing away from busy city life. Visiting these places helps reduce stress and allows people to enjoy activities like trekking, sightseeing, photography, and enjoying local food. The calm atmosphere and scenic views make hill stations an ideal destination for nature lovers and travelers who seek peace and freshness.


HISTORICAL PLACES

Historical places attract me because they reflect the culture, tradition, and history of a country. These places help us understand how people lived in the past and the importance of heritage preservation. Visiting historical monuments increases knowledge and creates respect for ancient architecture and craftsmanship. Exploring such places is both educational and inspiring, as they connect the present generation with the rich stories and achievements of earlier civilizations.
